Milestone 7 — Upload Failure Tracking
When a device reconnects and attempts to upload queued changes, some uploads may fail — through conflicts, interruptions, or validation rejection. This milestone detects, records, and surfaces those failures. The Conflict/Failed Management screen and the "Unsynced: Failed to Process" status chip give maintainers and supervisors visibility into what failed and what action is needed, closing a critical accountability gap before production.
Key deliverables · Jira-linked
  • Conflict/Failed Management screen on the FE DISCO-58
  • Sync error handling DISCO-59
  • "Unsynced: Failed to Process" status chip on the 781A DISCO-53
  • Upload Failure Report — review UI for failed uploads & follow-up off-board · not yet ticketed
Milestone 8 — RSO Deployment
The application — with all capability delivered through Milestone 7 — is deployed to the RSO environment by 31 December. At this point the system supports offline CAC authentication, pre-shift data sync, offline form actions across 781/781F/781H/781A, manual JCN generation, queued sync-back, and upload-failure reporting.
Key deliverable
  • Application deployed to RSO with full M1–M7 capability verified in production off-board
